Sarah Ahmed – IER 2022
Topic: Finding your first internship

In this conversation, Sarah shares her journey of landing her very first internship during the final year of her degree. We asked her about the challenges she faced while applying, what kind of portfolio helped her stand out, and how she overcame the fear of rejection during interviews.

Ali Khan – BSSE 2023
Topic: Dealing with exam pressure

Ali opens up about the stress many students feel during exam seasons and how he managed to build healthy study habits. We discussed questions like: How do you stay consistent when overwhelmed? What role do breaks and group studies play? And how can students avoid burnout while preparing for finals?

Maryam Iqbal – BSIT 2022
Topic: Balancing studies and personal life

Maryam reflects on her experiences balancing studies, part-time work, and personal responsibilities. We asked her how she planned her days, how she handled stress, and what advice she would give to students struggling to maintain balance in their academic and personal lives.
